Different Levels of Boots

There a 3 different versions of a football boots each for different levels. Academy boots are the cheapest and are normally for begginers. Pro boots are for people taking the sport more seriously and elite boot are the most expensive and are for the profesionals

Why football boots are important based on your positions and playstyle

Players who are fast will want lighter boots compare to a players who is more skillfull who would want a boot that would help them control the ball more
